About the platform

Transparency and trust for Ayurvedic herbal products through open standards.

Technology

Blockchain

Built on Hyperledger Fabric for permissioned, enterprise-grade provenance. Each event (CollectionEvent, ProcessingStep, QualityTest) is immutably recorded.

Data Interoperability

FHIR bundles standardize entities and enable secure data exchange with labs, manufacturers, and regulators.

Security

Role-based access, MSP identities, and audit trails ensure integrity without exposing private data.

Sustainability

Traceability spotlights responsible sourcing, fair trade practices, and biodiversity conservation.
